ABOUT CEYLON DIGITAL

Turning Vision Into Reality

Ceylon Digital is a creative studio focused on building high-performance websites, brands, and digital systems. We combine design, engineering, and strategy to help businesses grow online.

Built with intention, not templates.

We started Ceylon Digital with a simple idea — most websites look good, but don’t work well. Our focus is on clarity, performance, and long-term scalability.

About Ceylon Digital

Built on Collaboration, Driven by Craftsmanship

Ceylon Digital was founded by Madhushan Hemachandra, a software engineer, with a simple vision: to build a digital agency that values consistency, quality, and long-term thinking.

Built on a foundation of collaboration and shared craftsmanship, Ceylon Digital brought together designers, developers, and creative minds who believed great digital experiences require more than aesthetics alone. From the beginning, the focus has been on combining thoughtful design, strong engineering, and a clear understanding of business goals.

Based in Kandy, Sri Lanka, Ceylon Digital partners with growing brands, e-commerce businesses, startups, and service companies looking to strengthen their online presence. While our roots are in Sri Lanka, we also collaborate with clients internationally, bringing the same attention to detail and commitment to quality to every project.

Our expertise spans web design, UI/UX, custom website development, e-commerce solutions, SEO, branding, and ongoing support. With experience across technologies including Next.js, React, TypeScript, Node.js, Shopify, WordPress, Framer, and Webflow, we approach every project with a product mindset—focusing on performance, scalability, and conversion from day one.

At Ceylon Digital, we don't see websites as one-time deliverables. We see them as digital assets that should help businesses attract customers, build trust, and support long-term growth.

Our goal is simple: to raise the standard of digital experiences and build one of Sri Lanka's leading digital agencies—one project, one partnership, and one result at a time.

Meet the Founder

Madhushan Hemachandra

Madhushan Hemachandra is a software engineer specializing in frontend development, full-stack engineering, UI design, and system architecture. He founded Ceylon Digital with the belief that exceptional digital experiences emerge when design, technology, and business objectives work together.

Working alongside a growing network of talented designers and developers, he leads Ceylon Digital with a focus on craftsmanship, collaboration, and building digital products that help businesses grow with confidence.
