Launch and scale your online store with Ceylon Digital’s custom e-commerce solutions. We design and build secure, conversion-optimized storefronts with shopping cart functionality, payment gateways, inventory tools, and support for subscriptions and memberships.

What We Offer

Custom Storefront Design

  • Branded layout, fonts, and colors
  • Mobile-optimized design
  • Product and category page structure
  • User experience enhancements
Goal: Create a store that builds trust and drives conversions.

Shopping Cart Integration

  • Add-to-cart flow setup
  • Editable cart interface
  • Cart abandonment improvements
  • Checkout page optimization

  • Multi-currency support

  • Auto-update after purchases
  • Low-stock alerts
  • Back-in-stock tools

Subscriptions & Memberships

  • Monthly/quarterly product subscriptions
  • Recurring payments
  • Member-only discounts/content
  • Customer portals for account management
Goal: Build loyalty and stable, predictable revenue streams.
